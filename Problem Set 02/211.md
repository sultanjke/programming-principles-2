# Problem 211: 72871. Reverse elements from exttt{l} to exttt{r}

You’re given an array consisting of integers. Write a program that will reverse elements on the interval between positions *l* and *r*.

## Input format
The first line contains three integers *n*, *l*, *r*, (1 <= *n* <= 10^5, 1 <= *l* <= *r* <= *n*) — array size and positions *l* and *r*. The next line contains *n* integers $a_i$ (1 <= $a_i$ <= 10^9) — elements of the array.

## Output format
Array with reversed elements between positions *l* and *r*.

## Examples

### Input
5 2 5
2 8 10 5 12

### Output
2 12 5 10 8

### Input
6 4 6
8 12 8 12 2 2

### Output
8 12 8 2 2 12

### Input
6 4 5
12 13 15 5 12 13

### Output
12 13 15 12 5 13

### Input
9 8 9
5 12 10 11 14 3 14 1 11

### Output
5 12 10 11 14 3 14 11 1

### Input
5 1 5
11 2 13 9 1

### Output
1 9 13 2 11
